Before you start

Some things are reasons to see an ENT doctor and an audiologist before adjusting sound on your own:

  • one ear clearly worse than the other
  • tinnitus (ringing), ear drainage, or ear pain
  • dizziness or balance problems
  • sudden or one-sided hearing change

This extension is a listening-comfort EQ for streaming media. It is not a hearing aid, not a medical device, and not a substitute for professional hearing care.
